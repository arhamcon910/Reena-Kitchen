# Product Vision

**ID:** PM-VISION-001 | **v2.0** | **2026-07-06**

## The Vision

REENA is the world's first AI household operating system built specifically for Indian vegetarian families. It learns, remembers, and orchestrates every dimension of household life — starting with the kitchen.

## North Star Metric

**Meals where the family says "yes" without hesitation.** Target: 40% acceptance rate at week 4.

## The REENA Promise (4 weeks of daily use)

1. Know the household's top 3 preferred cuisines
2. Identify 5+ household pantry staples from cooking history
3. Align recommendations with all active health goals
4. Surface festival-appropriate meals without prompting
5. Recommend recipes cookable from current pantry

## Core Beliefs

- Indian families deserve AI built **for them**, not adapted from Western defaults
- Vegetarian is not a restriction — it is a rich, diverse, deeply cultural cuisine
- Privacy is non-negotiable — family data belongs to the family
- Trust is earned slowly — confidence builds as signals accumulate
- The **household is the unit** — not the individual, the recipe, or the ingredient
